      <h3>Oracle Accessibility News</h3> <a class="icn-img icn-plus cb105toggle-all"> <span class="cb105open-all">Open all</span> <span class="cb105close-all">Close all</span> </a> 
     </header> 
     <ul> 
      <li class="cb105open"> <h5>March 2021 Oracle Presentation and Sponsorship of CSUN</h5> 
       <div class="cb105w2"> 
        <p>Oracle was a sponsor of the <a href="https://www.csun.edu/cod/conference/sessions/2021" target="_blank">2021 Annual CSUN Assistive Technology Conference</a>, hosted by California State University, Northridge, March 2021. Michele Van Doozer of the Oracle Accessibility Program Office co-presented <a href="https://www.csun.edu/cod/conference/sessions/2021/index.php/public/presentations/view/1179" target="_blank">Accessibility Conformance Reports Using the VPAT</a> with IBM and HP. The presentation included introducing the <a href="https://www.itic.org/policy/accessibility/vpat-training" target="_blank">Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) Training</a> courses available from Information Technology Industry Council (ITI).</p> 
       </div> </li> 
      <li class="cb105open"> <h5>February 2021: European Disability Forum and Oracle e-Accessibility Scholarship 2021</h5> 
       <div class="cb105w2"> 
        <p>Applications are being accepted for the <a href="https://www.edf-feph.org/european-disability-forum-and-oracle-e-accessibility-scholarship-2021/" target="_blank">Oracle and European Disability Forum (EDF) scholarship</a> for students with disabilities studying in the field of Computer Science, Computer Engineering, User Experience, or related fields enrolled at a EU university for the academic year 2020-2021. Deadline to apply is March 6, 2020. The scholarship consists of 6,500€ to be awarded based on a project or research that will be conducted during the academic year. The Scholarship will be awarded primarily based on a project or thesis in which the needs of persons with disabilities in terms of accessibility to ICT (e-Accessibility) are taken into consideration and/or an innovative solution to enhance their access is proposed. The project must be an integral component of the educational program undertaken by the student (e.g. final year assignment or part of their Masters or PhD).</p> 
       </div> </li> 
      <li class="cb105open"> <h5>October 2020: European Disability Forum and Oracle e-Accessibility Scholarship Winner Announced</h5> 
       <div class="cb105w2"> 
        <p>Oracle and the European Disability Forum (EDF) announced Renata Deszo-Dinnyes as the winner of the 2019-2020 edition of the EDF-Oracle e-accessibility scholarship. This 6,500 euros award is to higher education students with disabilities in fields such as computer science, computer engineering, user experience and others. Renata's study focuses on user experience related to digitally manufactured objects, for example using a 3D printer to manufacture a prosthetic based on computer data. Full details of the scholarship and the awarding to Renata Deszo-Dinnyes can be found on <a href="http://www.edf-feph.org/newsroom/news/announcement-edf-and-oracle-award-scholarship-researcher-disability">Announcement - EDF and Oracle Award Scholarship to Researcher with Disability</a>.</p> 
       </div> </li> 
      <li class="cb105open"> <h5>March 2020: Oracle Presentations and Sponsorship of CSUN</h5> 
       <div class="cb105w2"> 
        <p>Oracle was an Executive Sponsor of the <a href="https://www.csun.edu/cod/conference/sessions/2020/">2020 Annual CSUN Assistive Technology Conference</a>, hosted by California State University, Northridge, March 10-13, 2020. Due to travel restrictions Oracle was unable to present at the conference this year. We look forward to the virtual conference next year. </p> 
       </div> </li>
